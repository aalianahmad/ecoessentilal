Our Material Selection Process

We follow a rigorous evaluation process to ensure every material meets our sustainability standards.

1

Research & Discovery

Our material specialists continuously research sustainable alternatives and emerging eco-friendly technologies to identify promising options.

2

Environmental Impact Assessment

Each potential material undergoes a comprehensive lifecycle analysis to evaluate its carbon footprint, water usage, and overall environmental impact.

3

Quality & Durability Testing

We rigorously test materials for durability, functionality, and longevity to ensure they can withstand real-world use and provide lasting value.

4

Supply Chain Verification

We trace materials back to their source, visiting suppliers and verifying that their practices align with our ethical and environmental standards.

5

Continuous Improvement

Even after implementation, we regularly review and refine our material choices as new information, technologies, and alternatives become available.
